KHM ‘22

Kochi Hub Meet (KHM) is the annual flagship event of IEEE Kochi Hub, providing a platform for Student Branches all across the Hub to connect, network, and learn together. Jam-packed with fun, creative, and innovative activities, KHM provides a premiere experience for its attendees in growing their technical as well as non-technical skills.That is at the premises of St. Joseph’s College of Engineering and Technology (SJCET), Palai. Together with SJCET, we are bringing up the most awaited carnival of this season.

IEEE SB SJCET PALAI (OUR HOST)

IEEE Student Branch of St. Joseph's College of Engineering and Technology, Palai is one of the most celebrated SBs in the Kerala Section. With a membership of 360 plus students, the SB has gained recognition at the section and region levels with awards like the Outstanding Student Branch Award in 2021, IEEE Regional Exemplary Student Branch Award in R-10 - 2020, Outstanding Student Branch award from Kochi Subsection in 2019, and IEEE PES HPSBCP Award. Since its induction, the SB has been organizing a wide variety of technical, and non-technical initiatives and humanitarian activities. These include technical symposiums, industry-led workshops, hands-on training programs, internships, industrial visits, and social projects. Through a series of well-initiated programs, the Student Branch enabled new IEEE members to work side by side with senior members, giving them a chance for growth and professional development. Over these years the IEEE SB SJCET has been working with a vision to upskill its members into highly qualified professionals.

IEEE Kochi Hub

IEEE Kochi Hub is one among the three hubs under IEEE LINK - the vibrant student activities community of IEEE Kerala Section. IEEE Kochi Hub consists of the student members from 44 different IEEE Student Branches across five districts - Kottayam, Idukki, Ernakulam, Alappuzha, and Thrissur. It has the top position for the highest number of student members, with over 3000. It plays a substantial role in bringing up various activities for the student community. IEEE Kochi Hub events are famous for their vibrancy. IEEE Kochi Hub aims to help its student members grow technically and professionally with the help of impactful events and activities.
